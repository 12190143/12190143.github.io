About me
Greetings! I'm a 4th year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Liang He at East China Normal University. My research interests are sentiment analysis, deep learning and natural language processing. Before graduation, I worked as an International Visiting Research Trainee in the lab of Information Retrieval and Knowledge Management in York University, Canada (2018.10-2019.10).
